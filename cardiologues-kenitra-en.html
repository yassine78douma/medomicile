When should you see a cardiologist?

A cardiologist is a doctor specializing in the heart and blood vessels. A consultation may be useful for chest pain, palpitations, unusual shortness of breath, severe fatigue during effort or high blood pressure. It may also be recommended after fainting, loss of consciousness, a known heart murmur, or a test result that needs a specialist opinion.

Cardiology follow-up may also concern people who have already had a heart attack, heart surgery, angioplasty, valve disease, rhythm disorder or heart failure. Patients with several cardiovascular risk factors, such as hypertension, diabetes, smoking, high cholesterol, excess weight or family history, may need assessment or regular follow-up.

This directory simply helps you find contact details for cardiologists practicing in Kenitra. The information does not replace medical advice, does not make a diagnosis and does not provide treatment. Before going, contact the practice to confirm the address, opening hours, availability and appointment conditions.

In case of severe chest pain, breathing distress, serious fainting or suspected cardiovascular emergency, contact emergency services immediately.
