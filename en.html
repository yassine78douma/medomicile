Which on-duty pharmacy is near me in Kenitra or Mehdia?

Most pharmacies in Kenitra are open from 9 a.m. to 8 p.m., Monday to Saturday. However, it can be difficult to find one open on Sunday or on public holidays. It is useful to keep on-duty pharmacy contact details close at hand when needed. You can usually find these details in pharmacy windows, on our site, or by contacting medical centers and clinics.
